Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for upcoming sports events?

You can register for upcoming events through our Events page. Look for events with open registration and click the "Register" button to fill out the registration form.

How can athletes apply for sports scholarships?

Athletes can apply for sports scholarships through the Scholarships section on our website or by contacting the Department of Sports directly. Eligibility criteria and application procedures are detailed on the scholarship page.

What are the requirements to join the national team?

To join the national team, athletes must participate in national-level tournaments and trials. Selection is based on performance, fitness, and potential. Specific requirements vary by sport and are determined by the respective national sports federations.

How can I provide feedback on your services?

You can provide feedback through the contact form on this page, by sending an email to feedback@sports.gov.in, or by calling our office directly at +91-11-XXXXXXXX.
