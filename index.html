About the Department

The Department of Sports functions under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India. Our mission is to develop and promote sports throughout the country, identify and nurture sporting talent, provide world-class facilities, and ensure India's success in international sports competitions.

We work closely with national sports federations, state governments, and various stakeholders to create a robust sports ecosystem that enables athletes to excel on the global stage.
